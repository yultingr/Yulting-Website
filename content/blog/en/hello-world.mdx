# Hello World

Welcome to my new personal website and blog! I am happy to share this space with you.

## Why I Created This Site

I wanted a place to share my reflections on Buddhist philosophy, translation work, and the journey of bringing Tibetan wisdom to a wider audience. This website gives me a space to:

- Share insights from my studies and teachings
- Write about the art of English-Tibetan translation
- Connect with students, scholars, and anyone curious about Buddhist thought

## What You'll Find Here

This site includes information about my work as an educator and translator at Gaden Shartse Monastery, along with videos of teachings and talks, and blog posts where I hope to explore topics at the intersection of traditional Buddhist scholarship and modern education.

## What's Next

I plan to write about Buddhist philosophy, the challenges and joys of translation, and the importance of bridging contemplative practice with contemporary knowledge. Stay tuned!

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find something meaningful here.
